Optimized Instagram Experience

PawGram is a performance-focused Instagram client engineered to deliver improved responsiveness, enhanced operational stability, and consistent built-in emoji rendering. Designed for both legacy and modern Android architectures (32-bit & 64-bit), PawGram emphasizes efficiency, reliability, and structured community-driven development.
